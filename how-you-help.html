How You Help

At Beyond Blue, we accept all kinds of help, whether that be through active service and volunteering work, to a simple donation. Everyone can do their part to make the world a better place.


For example, in 2018-2019, $12.6 million was donated to Beyond Blue from people just like you through fundraising efforts, corporate partnerships and philanthropic gifts, as well through donations. Fundraisers are an especially effective way of raising awareness for the cause.


We run different campaigns throughout the year, highlighting a particular area of mental health we believe needs more attention. This month, we are targetting LGTBI mental health awareness, as seen by our front page.


You can donated to a specific cause that resonates the most strongly with you, or click on the donation tab above to donate immediatly to our current LGBTI awareness initiative.
